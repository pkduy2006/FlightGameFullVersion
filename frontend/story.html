User Story

Your country is in a war with another country. You are a combat pilot, and you always want to destroy the enemy’s infrastructure as much as you can. One day, you have an ultimate mission: destroy the enemy's main airports.

You set off on your journey from your base. You will be provided with a bomber jet and a limited amount of aviation gasoline. Be careful with it, because you will absolutely be killed by your enemy if you run out of fuel while completing your mission.

When you arrive at the airspace of any airport, you are greeted by a message from your control display board. After that, you will receive a notification that makes you choose either to destroy or land at this airport. Both choices have advantages and disadvantages. So, be careful when making any decisions!

When you arrive at your target, you must destroy it. You will receive a huge bonus after finishing your mission.

Then, you have the last mission: Come home safely. Although the enemy’s main airport is destroyed, they are still very strong. As a result, do not relax.
